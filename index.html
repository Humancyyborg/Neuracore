Neuracore — We develops seizure prediction technology by analyzing EEG brain wave data

Neuracore is developing AI-powered wearable sensors that predict seizures before they occur. Our advanced algorithms analyze EEG data to identify unusual brain activity, providing valuable time for users to take precautions and prepare for an impending seizure.

  • Wearable EEG sensor band powered by proprietary AI algorithms
  • Analyzes brain activity in real-time to predict seizures before they occur
  • Provides user with 15-20 minute advanced warning of an impending seizure
