Cortex Lab

Neural innovation research. A small lab investigating neural stem cell therapy and AI-modeled epilepsy treatment.

Research Brief

We're a small research lab investigating the use of neural stem cells to treat epilepsy. We build AI models of epilepsy to test the delivery of stem cells to the brain.

Our goals: suppress seizures, repair damaged brain regions, and understand how stem cells connect and interact with the brain's existing networks.
