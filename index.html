Cortex Lab

Neural Innovation Research

We're a small research lab investigating the use of neural stem cells to treat epilepsy. We develop AI models of epilepsy to test the delivery of stem cells to the brain.

Our goals are to suppress seizures, repair damaged brain regions, and understand how stem cells connect and interact with the brain's existing networks.

Projects

Aurevia

While we are research-focused, we are also building Aurevia—a device that monitors brainwave patterns to detect seizures 15 minutes before they occur—to provide immediate help to people suffering from seizures.

Ada

A modular computing stack designed to host local open-source models, requiring no internet. No internet, no data, no problem. We envision this becoming an agentic brain in the future, but the lab is currently building it to provide advanced open-source models to children in parts of the world without internet access or where connectivity is expensive.
